The "Kiel Model" is live. The first digital birth notifications have been transmitted machine-to-machine and received by the civil registry office — ending a process that relied on paper for nearly 150 years.

Vertama provided the technical infrastructure enabling this end-to-end M2M process, delivered jointly with Städtisches Krankenhaus Kiel (SKK) and Universitätsklinikum Schleswig-Holstein (UKSH). Notifications are transmitted directly from the hospital information system to the registry office, with no manual handoff.

**Rolling out nationally**

Klinikum Region Hannover has already adopted the process. Further regions have expressed interest. The result validates Vertama's approach: an open, KIS-agnostic solution that scales beyond any single hospital or region.

Vertama's DiGG product provides the technical foundation: XPersonenstand standard, OSCI infrastructure, seamless KIS integration. The source article is in German.
