**Oracle has listed Vertama in a press release covering companies expanding their solutions for Oracle EU Sovereign Cloud. In practice, this means hospitals that run — or plan to run — their infrastructure on Oracle EU Sovereign Cloud can now deploy Vertama's reporting process solutions in that environment.**

Vertama takes a platform-agnostic approach. The choice of cloud infrastructure sits with the hospital — Vertama adapts to the client's environment, not the other way around. Expanding to Oracle EU Sovereign Cloud is not a strategic shift; it is an extension of the options available to clients for whom data sovereignty and EU infrastructure residency carry particular weight.
