**From April 21 to 23, 2026, Vertama will be present at DMEA in Berlin. The SAP Healthcare Partner Initiative lists Vertama as a partner and will have its own stand at Hall 4.2, C-113.**

DMEA is Europe's leading conference and trade fair for digital health. Vertama is part of the SAP Healthcare Partner network and builds its reporting process solutions on SAP Business Technology Platform.

Vertama will also be present in person at the Berlin Partner joint stand (Hall 3.2, Stand D-103).
