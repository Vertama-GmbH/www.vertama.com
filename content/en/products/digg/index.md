## DiGG – Digital birth registration

Birth registration at the registry office is legally required and time-critical. DiGG fully digitalizes this process – from the delivery room to the authority.

### No more paperwork in the delivery room

No more handwritten forms. No more faxes. DiGG handles:
- Automatic data transfer from the HIS
- Digital completion of missing information
- Encrypted transmission to the registry office
- Delivery receipt and archiving

### Directly from the patient record

DiGG integrates seamlessly into your delivery room workflow:
- "Birth notification" button in the patient record
- Form is already pre-filled
- Only add missing information
- One click to submit

### XPersonenstand standard

DiGG uses the official XPersonenstand data standard:
- Developed by federal and state authorities
- Uniform nationwide
- Future-proof
- Fully legally compliant (§ 18 Para. 1 PStG)

### OSCI infrastructure

Transmission is via the OSCI communication infrastructure:
- Officially recognized standard
- End-to-end encrypted
- Legally secure delivery receipt
- Audit-proof logging

### Time savings for your midwife team

**Before:**
- Handwritten form completion
- Fax to registry office (often illegible)
- Follow-up calls for missing information
- Time: 20–30 minutes per birth

**With DiGG:**
- Data already pre-filled
- Only add missing information
- Digital transmission with confirmation
- Time: 2–5 minutes per birth
- **Saving: up to 25 minutes per birth**

### No more follow-up queries

Structured data capture and mandatory field validation prevent common errors:
- Missing information is identified immediately
- Plausibility check
- Complete transmission
- Fewer queries from the registry office

### Technical integration

DiGG integrates into your delivery room IT:
- SAP IS-H / i.s.h.med
- Oracle Health (Cerner)
- ORBIS
- viewTEX
- Further birth modules on request

### Data protection & security

Birth notifications contain sensitive personal data. DiGG protects this through:
- End-to-end encryption
- No permanent central storage
- Access control and audit logs
- SAP Business Technology Platform (SOC 2, BSI C5)
- Federal OSCI infrastructure
